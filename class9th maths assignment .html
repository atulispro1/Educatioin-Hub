Class 9 Maths Important Assignment Questions

Practice these important questions to strengthen your Maths skills!

Important Questions to Practice

  • Find the value of x in the following linear equation: 3x - 4 = 11
  • Solve the quadratic equation: x² - 5x + 6 = 0
  • Factorize: x² + 5x + 6
  • Find the area of a triangle with base 12 cm and height 8 cm.
  • Find the circumference of a circle with radius 7 cm. (Use π = 22/7)
  • What is the value of sin 30°? Derive the value.
  • Prove that the sum of the angles of a triangle is 180°.
  • Find the HCF and LCM of 36 and 60 using Euclid’s division lemma.
  • If the probability of an event is 1/4, find the odds against it.
  • Calculate the surface area of a cylinder with radius 4 cm and height 10 cm.
  • If the sum of the first n natural numbers is given by S = n(n+1)/2, find the sum for n = 100.
  • Find the distance between the points (2, 3) and (5, 7).
  • Find the volume of a cone with radius 3 cm and height 8 cm. (Use π = 22/7)
  • Find the median of the following data: 2, 5, 8, 10, 12, 15, 18.
  • If the area of a rectangle is 60 cm² and its length is 12 cm, find the width of the rectangle.
  • Solve for x: 5(x - 2) = 3(x + 4)
  • Find the mean of the following data: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50.
  • Find the mode of the following data: 2, 3, 5, 5, 6, 6, 6, 8.
  • In a right-angled triangle, if one leg is 6 cm and the hypotenuse is 10 cm, find the other leg.
  • Prove that the sum of the squares of any two consecutive natural numbers is always odd.
  • Find the solution set for the system of equations: x + y = 7 and 2x - y = 5.
  • If the ratio of the areas of two squares is 9:16, find the ratio of their sides.
  • In a circle, the radius is 14 cm. Find the area of the circle. (Use π = 22/7)
  • Solve for y: 2y + 3 = 7y - 5
  • Find the perimeter of a rectangle with length 15 cm and breadth 10 cm.
  • Find the value of tan 45° and verify the trigonometric identity: 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ.
  • Find the volume of a cuboid with length 5 cm, width 3 cm, and height 8 cm.
  • Solve for x: x² + 6x + 9 = 0.
  • Find the slope of the line passing through the points (2, 3) and (5, 7).
  • Find the area of a sector of a circle with radius 10 cm and central angle 60°.
  • Find the roots of the quadratic equation: x² - 4x + 3 = 0.
  • If the sum of the angles of a quadrilateral is 360°, prove that the sum of the opposite angles is equal.
  • Find the roots of the equation: 3x² + 8x - 3 = 0.
