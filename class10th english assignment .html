Class 10 English Important Assignment Questions

Practice your literature, grammar, and writing skills!

Important Questions

  • Summarize the poem “The Road Not Taken” in your own words.
  • Explain the central theme of “Dust of Snow.”
  • Describe the character sketch of Nelson Mandela from “Long Walk to Freedom.”
  • What message does the story “A Letter to God” convey?
  • How did Anne Frank describe her life in her diary?
  • Discuss the role of the tiger in the poem “A Tiger in the Zoo.”
  • What was the main conflict in “The Proposal” and how was it resolved?
  • Write a letter to your principal requesting extra classes before exams.
  • Write an article on the importance of reading books.
  • Explain the rules of subject-verb agreement with examples.
  • Transform the following sentences from active to passive voice.
  • Write a story with the following opening: “It was a dark and stormy night...”
  • Correct the errors in the following sentences.
  • Describe the poet’s attitude in “Fire and Ice.”
  • Explain the use of irony in “The Proposal.”
