Class 8 Maths Important Assignment Questions

Practice these important questions to strengthen your skills!

Important Questions to Practice

  • Simplify the following expressions:
    • 3x + 5 + 7x - 2
    • 5a - 3b + 2a + b
  • Evaluate the expression when x = 4:
    • 3x + 2
    • 4x - 7
  • Factorize the following expressions:
    • x² + 7x + 12
    • 3y² + 10y + 7
  • Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 15
  • Solve for y: 3y - 4 = 8
  • Solve for z: 5z + 3 = 23
  • Find the value of x: 4x - 3 = 2x + 7
  • Find the perimeter of a triangle with sides 5 cm, 6 cm, and 7 cm.
  • Calculate the volume of a cylinder with radius 4 cm and height 10 cm.
  • Find the surface area of a cone with radius 7 cm and height 14 cm.
  • Find the volume of a cone with radius 3 cm and height 9 cm.
  • Draw a bar graph to represent the following data:
    • 5, 8, 10, 12, 6, 7
  • Calculate the mean of the following numbers:
    • 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18
  • Find the mode and median for the data set:
    • 5, 7, 9, 9, 10, 12
  • What is the probability of drawing a red card from a deck of 52 cards?
  • Find the probability of getting a number greater than 4 when a die is rolled.
  • If a bag contains 3 red balls, 2 green balls, and 5 blue balls, what is the probability of drawing a green ball?
  • Calculate the surface area of a cube with side length 5 cm.
  • Calculate the range of the following numbers:
    • 4, 7, 10, 14, 20
