Class 10 Maths Important Assignment Questions

Practice key problems and strengthen your concepts!

Important Questions

  • Find the roots of the quadratic equation: x² - 5x + 6 = 0.
  • Solve the pair of linear equations: 2x + 3y = 12, x - y = 4.
  • Prove that √2 is an irrational number.
  • Find the HCF and LCM of 96 and 404 by the prime factorization method.
  • Find the 10th term of the arithmetic progression (AP): 2, 5, 8, 11, …
  • Find the sum of the first 20 terms of the AP: 7, 13, 19, 25, …
  • Draw a graph for the equation y = 2x + 3.
  • Calculate the area of a triangle with sides 7 cm, 24 cm, and 25 cm.
  • Find the volume and surface area of a cone with height 10 cm and radius 5 cm.
  • Write the equation of a line parallel to y = 3x + 2.
  • Using the quadratic formula, solve 3x² + 2x - 1 = 0.
  • Construct a triangle similar to a given triangle with a scale factor of 3/2.
  • Find the coordinates of the centroid of the triangle with vertices (2,3), (4,5), (6,7).
  • Find the mean, median, and mode of the data: 5, 7, 8, 9, 9, 10, 11.
  • Calculate the probability of getting a head when tossing a coin.
  • Find the distance between points (3,4) and (7,1).
  • Calculate the length of the diagonal of a rectangle with sides 8 cm and 15 cm.
  • Prove that the tangents drawn from an external point to a circle are equal in length.
  • Find the equation of the circle with center (0,0) and radius 5.
  • Calculate the surface area and volume of a hemisphere with radius 7 cm.
  • Solve: If sin A = 3/5, find cos A and tan A.
  • Evaluate: sin²30° + cos²30°.
  • Find the sum of the zeros and the product of the zeros of the polynomial 2x² - 3x + 1.
  • Calculate the total surface area of a cylinder of radius 4 cm and height 10 cm.
