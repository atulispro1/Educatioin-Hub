Class 8 English Important Assignment Questions

Practice these important questions to enhance your understanding of English!

Important Questions to Practice

  • Write a summary of the following poem/story:
    • “The Road Not Taken” by Robert Frost
    • “The Gift of the Magi” by O. Henry
  • Answer the following questions based on the story:
    • What was the moral of “The Gift of the Magi”?
    • Explain the theme of “The Road Not Taken.”
  • Write a letter to your friend describing your summer vacation.
  • Write an essay on the topic: “The Importance of Time Management in Student Life.”
  • Make sentences with the following words:
    • Enthusiastic
    • Determined
    • Grateful
  • Change the following sentences into indirect speech:
    • She said, “I will go to the market tomorrow.”
    • He said, “I am doing my homework now.”
  • Change the following sentences into active voice:
    • The letter was written by John.
    • The cake was baked by my mother.
  • Fill in the blanks with appropriate prepositions:
    • She walked __ the park.
    • He is sitting __ the table.
  • Choose the correct article (a, an, the) for the following sentences:
    • He is __ honest person.
    • This is __ best movie I have ever seen.
  • Write an article on "The Impact of Social Media on Youth."
  • Complete the following story:
    • Once there was a small village where everyone lived happily...
  • Write a paragraph on “My Favorite Festival.”
  • Identify the figure of speech in the following sentences:
    • “Time is money.”
    • “The wind whispered through the trees.”
  • Write a dialogue between two friends on how to spend the weekend.
  • Write the past tense of the following verbs:
    • Go
    • Run
    • Buy
  • Write the opposite of the following adjectives:
    • Happy
    • Good
    • Beautiful
  • Make an outline for the essay on "The Advantages of Online Education."
