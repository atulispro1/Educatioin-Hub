Class 10 Science Important Assignment Questions

Revise deeply and master every concept!

Important Questions

  • State Ohm's law and explain how to verify it experimentally.
  • Draw a labeled diagram of the human heart and explain blood circulation.
  • Explain the process of photosynthesis with a neat diagram.
  • Define acid, base, and salt. Give two examples of each.
  • What is the modern periodic table? State its main features.
  • Explain the types of chemical reactions with examples.
  • Discuss the formation of the image in a convex lens and its uses.
  • Describe the structure and function of the human brain.
  • What is a homologous series? Give examples.
  • Differentiate between AC and DC current with diagrams.
  • State the laws of reflection and refraction with diagrams.
  • What is sustainable development? Why is it important?
  • Describe the role of the small intestine in digestion.
  • Define magnetic field and draw the magnetic field lines around a bar magnet.
  • Explain the process of corrosion and methods to prevent it.
  • State the differences between covalent and ionic bonds.
  • What are fossils? Describe their importance in evolution.
  • Describe the working of an electric motor with a neat diagram.
  • Explain Mendel's experiment on pea plants and the law of segregation.
  • Write the differences between metals and non-metals with examples.
  • Describe the nitrogen cycle with the help of a diagram.
  • What is a parallel circuit? State its advantages over a series circuit.
  • Explain the differences between biodegradable and non-biodegradable substances.
  • Write a short note on the greenhouse effect and global warming.
