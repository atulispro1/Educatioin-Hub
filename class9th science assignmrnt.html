Class 9 Science Important Assignment Questions

Practice these important questions to strengthen your Science knowledge!

Important Questions to Practice

  • What is the structure of an atom? Describe its parts with the help of diagrams.
  • Explain the law of conservation of mass with an example.
  • Differentiate between physical and chemical changes with examples.
  • Write the difference between elements, compounds, and mixtures with examples.
  • Explain the process of photosynthesis in plants with chemical equations.
  • What is Newton’s First Law of Motion? Explain it with examples.
  • Define acceleration and derive the equation of motion for uniformly accelerated motion.
  • What is the significance of the atomic number and mass number? Explain with examples.
  • Describe the process of respiration in plants and animals.
  • Explain the concept of energy transformation with examples from daily life.
  • What is the role of enzymes in the human digestive system?
  • Describe the different types of reproduction in plants.
  • Explain how the cell division process occurs during mitosis and meiosis.
  • What are the three main types of heat transfer? Explain each with examples.
  • What is the difference between latent heat and specific heat capacity? Provide examples.
  • Explain the different types of forces acting on a body and how they affect motion.
  • Describe the concept of pressure and how it is measured.
  • What are acids, bases, and salts? Explain their properties with examples.
  • What is the importance of water in the environment? Explain the water cycle.
  • Describe the structure and functions of the human heart.
  • What is the difference between conductors and insulators? Provide examples.
  • Explain the various methods of separating mixtures, with examples.
  • What are fossils? Explain how fossils are formed and their importance in understanding Earth’s history.
  • Discuss the applications of magnets and magnetic fields in daily life.
  • What is the difference between renewable and non-renewable resources? Explain with examples.
  • What is the concept of work and power in physics? How are they related?
  • Explain the process of burning fuels and the factors affecting combustion.
  • What is the electromagnetic spectrum? Explain its various types with examples.
  • Define the terms diffraction and refraction. Explain the differences with examples.
  • What is an electric current? Discuss the effects of electric current on a conductor.
  • What is the principle of a lever? Explain the different types of levers with examples.
  • Describe the properties of light and its behavior when passing through different mediums.
  • What is the role of water in maintaining life on Earth? Discuss the importance of water conservation.
  • What are the effects of pollution on human health and the environment? Discuss prevention methods.
  • What is climate change and how does it affect the Earth’s atmosphere and living organisms?
  • What are the uses of biotechnology in medicine and agriculture?
  • Explain the role of the endocrine system in human beings.
  • What are the various sources of energy and their impact on the environment?
  • What is the importance of nutrition in human health? Explain the nutrients required for a balanced diet.
  • How do living organisms maintain homeostasis? Explain with examples.
  • Describe the role of the nervous system in the human body.
  • What is the relationship between force, mass, and acceleration? Explain using Newton’s second law of motion.
  • Explain the term ‘work-energy theorem’ and provide an example from daily life.
  • What is the process of nuclear fission and fusion? Explain with examples.
