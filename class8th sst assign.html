Class 8 Social Science (SST) Important Assignment Questions

Practice these important questions to strengthen your Social Science knowledge!

Important Questions to Practice

  • What are the main features of the Harappan Civilization?
  • Explain the causes of the First War of Indian Independence (1857).
  • What is the role of the British East India Company in the establishment of British rule in India?
  • Write about the social and economic impact of British rule on India.
  • Describe the impact of the Industrial Revolution in Europe.
  • What were the main causes of the French Revolution?
  • Explain the significance of the Magna Carta in the development of democracy.
  • What is meant by Nationalism? Explain its role in the independence movement of India.
  • Describe the causes and effects of World War I.
  • What were the objectives of the United Nations Organization (UNO)?
  • Explain the causes of the fall of the Mughal Empire.
  • What is the importance of the Indian National Congress in the freedom movement?
  • Explain the role of Mahatma Gandhi in the Indian Independence Movement.
  • Describe the role of Subhas Chandra Bose and the Indian National Army in the freedom struggle.
  • What is meant by the “Divide and Rule” policy of the British? Explain its impact on India.
  • What are the key features of the Indian Constitution?
  • Explain the concept of secularism as mentioned in the Indian Constitution.
  • What is the significance of the Right to Equality in the Indian Constitution?
  • Discuss the role of the judiciary in the Indian democracy.
  • Explain the differences between the French Revolution and the American Revolution.
  • What is the Green Revolution? Explain its impact on Indian agriculture.
  • Explain the importance of the non-alignment movement during the Cold War era.
  • What is the significance of the Quit India Movement in India’s independence struggle?
  • Write a short note on the contribution of Jawaharlal Nehru to the Indian freedom movement.
  • What were the effects of British colonial rule on the Indian economy?
  • Describe the role of Rani Lakshmibai in the 1857 revolt.
  • What were the causes of the partition of India in 1947?
  • What are the major landforms of the Earth? Explain each with examples.
  • What is the importance of rivers in the economy of a country? Give examples.
  • Describe the role of forests in maintaining ecological balance.
  • What are natural resources? Explain the importance of conserving them.
  • What are the causes and effects of air pollution? Suggest measures to reduce it.
  • Explain the concept of global warming and its environmental impact.
  • Describe the role of agriculture in the Indian economy.
  • What are the major industries in India? Explain their importance.
  • Discuss the role of tourism in the economy of India.
  • What is the importance of transport and communication in a country’s development?
  • What are the effects of deforestation on the environment and human life?
  • Explain the concept of democracy and its features.
  • What are the different types of government systems in the world? Explain briefly.
  • Describe the role of women in the Indian freedom struggle.
  • Explain the significance of the battle of Plassey in the history of India.
  • What are the effects of British rule on Indian society and culture?
  • What is the importance of the Indian independence movement in the world context?
