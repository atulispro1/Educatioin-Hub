Chapter 1: The Fun They Had

Summary: Set in 2157, Margie and Tommy discover an old book about past schools. Margie dislikes her mechanical teacher and wonders how fun old schools with human teachers were.

Themes: Technology vs. human connection, joy of shared learning.

Characters: Margie, Tommy, Mechanical Teacher.

Important Points:

  • Margie's poor marks; County Inspector fixes teacher.
  • Tommy explains human teachers and group learning.
  • Margie dreams of the fun old schools.

Questions: Why did Margie hate school? What fascinated them? What’s the message?

Chapter 2: The Sound of Music

Part 1 — Evelyn Glennie: Deaf musician, feels vibrations, world-famous percussionist, proves determination defeats disability.

Part 2 — Bismillah Khan: Shehnai maestro, played on Independence Day, loved Indian culture over fame abroad.

Chapter 3: The Little Girl

Kezia fears her father but understands his love when he cares for her during her mother’s illness. Theme: Love can hide behind strict behavior.

Chapter 4: A Truly Beautiful Mind

Einstein’s journey from a curious child to a global genius and peace activist. Message: Greatness comes from intelligence + humanity.

Chapter 5: The Snake and the Mirror

Humorous tale of a doctor, his vanity, and a snake encounter. Teaches humility and life’s unpredictability.

Chapter 6: My Childhood

APJ Abdul Kalam’s childhood, simplicity, hard work, and values of harmony and perseverance from his family.

Chapter 7: Packing

Comic disaster when three friends pack for a trip. Overconfidence leads to mistakes. Theme: Self-awareness and humor in chaos.

Chapter 8: Reach for the Top

Santosh Yadav: Mountaineer, Everest climber, challenged stereotypes.

Maria Sharapova: Tennis star, rose from tough childhood, symbol of grit.

Chapter 9: The Bond of Love

Bruno the bear and his loving human family. Emotional journey, separation, and reunion. Highlights human-animal bond.

Chapter 10: Kathmandu

Travel piece on Nepal’s temples, streets, sounds, and scenes. Highlights cultural diversity and beauty.

Chapter 11: If I Were You

Drama of a playwright outsmarting a criminal. Theme: Intelligence and presence of mind save the day.
