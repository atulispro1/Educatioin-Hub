Class 10 SST Important Assignment Questions

History, Geography, Civics, Economics — All in one place!

Important Questions

  • Explain the major outcomes of the French Revolution.
  • Discuss the factors responsible for the rise of nationalism in India.
  • Describe the Non-Cooperation Movement and its impact.
  • Explain the major soil types of India and their distribution.
  • What are the key features of Indian agriculture?
  • Describe the Indian monsoon and its significance.
  • Explain the importance of water resources and conservation methods.
  • Discuss the importance of democracy in a diverse country like India.
  • Differentiate between power-sharing in Belgium and Sri Lanka.
  • Explain the working of federalism in India with examples.
  • Discuss the challenges to democracy and how they can be overcome.
  • Explain the concept of sustainable development with examples.
  • Describe the formal and informal sectors of the Indian economy.
  • What are the reasons for poverty in India? Explain government measures to reduce it.
  • Discuss the role of globalization and its impact on India.
  • Explain the significance of the service sector in the Indian economy.
  • Describe the role of political parties in a democratic country.
  • Explain the role of the Election Commission in India.
  • Discuss the impact of consumer rights in improving market practices.
  • Describe the main objectives of the five-year plans in India.
  • Write a note on the National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (NREGA).
  • Explain the term ‘money’ and the modern forms of money.
  • Discuss the causes and effects of global warming.
  • Describe any two social reform movements in 19th century India.
