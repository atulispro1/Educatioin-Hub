Class 9 SST Important Assignment Questions

Covering History, Geography, Civics, and Economics — a complete practice set for exams!

Important Questions to Practice

  • Explain the main causes of the French Revolution.
  • Describe the rise of Hitler and the Nazi party in Germany.
  • What were the effects of colonialism on Indian agriculture?
  • Explain the physical features of India with diagrams.
  • Describe the major soil types found in India and their characteristics.
  • What is the significance of the Indian monsoon?
  • Discuss the importance of natural vegetation and wildlife conservation.
  • Explain the role of democracy in ensuring human rights.
  • Differentiate between a democratic and a non-democratic government with examples.
  • What is the role of the Parliament in India?
  • Explain the process of making a law in the Indian Parliament.
  • Describe the challenges to democracy in the modern world.
  • Explain poverty as seen in India. What are its main causes?
  • Discuss the problem of unemployment in India and suggest remedies.
  • Explain the concept of sustainable development.
  • What are the major sectors of the Indian economy?
  • Discuss the Green Revolution and its impact on Indian agriculture.
  • Explain the importance of public and private sectors in India.
  • Describe the role of the government in reducing inequality in society.
  • Discuss the role of globalization in the Indian economy.
  • Write a short note on the National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (NREGA).
  • Describe the impact of climate change on agriculture.
  • Explain the differences between developed and developing countries.
  • Discuss the significance of the Indian Constitution.
  • What are fundamental rights and duties?
  • Explain the role of local government in India (Panchayati Raj).
  • Describe India’s foreign policy objectives since independence.
  • What is federalism? Explain with examples from India.
  • Discuss the role of women in Indian freedom struggle.
  • Explain the importance of secularism in India.
