Class 9 English Important Assignment Questions

Practice these important questions to strengthen your English language and literature skills!

Important Questions to Practice

  • Write an essay on 'The Importance of Education'.
  • What are the central themes in the story 'The Last Leaf' by O. Henry?
  • Write a letter to your friend describing your recent vacation.
  • Explain the significance of the poem 'The Road Not Taken' by Robert Frost.
  • What are the main ideas presented in the chapter 'A Truly Beautiful Mind'?
  • Write a paragraph on the topic 'My Favorite Book'.
  • Discuss the character sketch of 'Dr. Sadao' from 'The Enemy'.
  • What is the role of imagination in the story 'The Adventures of Toto' by Ruskin Bond?
  • Write a letter to the editor of a newspaper on the topic 'Pollution in Our City'.
  • Explain the figure of speech used in the poem 'The Ball Poem' by John Berryman.
  • What message does the poem 'A Photograph' by Shirley Toulson convey?
  • Write an essay on 'The Importance of Sports in Our Life'.
  • What is the moral of the story 'The Hack Driver' by Sinclair Lewis?
  • What are the main features of the story 'The Thief’s Story' by Ruskin Bond?
  • Write a letter to your principal requesting for permission to hold an inter-school competition.
  • Explain the role of 'The Great Stone Face' in the story by Nathaniel Hawthorne.
  • Discuss the role of technology in human life in the context of 'The Story of My Life' by Helen Keller.
  • Write a paragraph on 'The Changing Role of Women in Society'.
  • Discuss the characters of 'A Tiger in the Zoo' by Leslie Norris.
  • Write a summary of the story 'The Lost Child' by Mulk Raj Anand.
  • What is the significance of the character 'Vikram' in the poem 'The Tale of the Bamboo Cutter'?
  • What are the similarities and differences between the two poems 'Wind' and 'Rain on the Roof'?
  • Discuss the influence of the 'Renaissance' on the development of English literature.
  • What are the various types of sentence structures in English? Provide examples.
  • Write an article on 'The Role of Social Media in Modern Society'.
  • Explain the use of symbolism in 'The Eyes Have It' by Ruskin Bond.
  • What is the central message of the story 'The Happy Prince' by Oscar Wilde?
  • Write a dialogue between two friends about the importance of teamwork in school projects.
