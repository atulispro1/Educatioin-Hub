Class 8 Science Important Assignment Questions

Practice these important questions to boost your science understanding!

Important Questions to Practice

  • Define the following terms:
    • Atoms
    • Mixtures
    • Solubility
    • Acids and Bases
  • Write the properties of matter.
  • Explain the three states of matter with examples.
  • What is the law of conservation of mass? Explain with an example.
  • Describe the process of filtration and give an example where it is used.
  • What are the physical and chemical properties of water?
  • What is photosynthesis? Write its equation.
  • Explain the importance of water cycle in nature.
  • Differentiate between plant and animal cells.
  • What is the structure of the human digestive system? Explain each part briefly.
  • Write the difference between kinetic and potential energy.
  • What are the types of energy and give examples for each?
  • Explain the process of respiration in plants.
  • What is the difference between exothermic and endothermic reactions?
  • Define inertia. How does it affect motion?
  • What are the types of forces? Explain with examples.
  • What is a chemical reaction? Write an example of a chemical reaction in daily life.
  • What is the difference between soluble and insoluble substances?
  • Explain the method of preparing a salt by evaporation.
  • What is an ecosystem? Describe the various components of an ecosystem.
  • Write the differences between renewable and non-renewable resources.
  • Explain how the human skeleton works and its importance.
  • What are the functions of the heart and how does it pump blood?
  • Draw and explain the human circulatory system.
  • What is energy transfer? Explain the different types of energy transfer.
  • Define density. How does density affect the floating and sinking of objects?
  • What is the law of reflection? How does it work?
  • Explain the types of lenses and their uses.
  • Write an experiment to demonstrate the formation of shadows.
  • What is force? Write its types and explain each with examples.
  • Differentiate between plant reproduction and animal reproduction.
  • Write the steps of digestion in humans.
  • Explain the role of enzymes in digestion.
  • What is an electric circuit? Draw and label a simple circuit.
  • What are the types of current? Explain AC and DC.
  • What is a magnet? Explain its properties and uses.
  • Write the differences between natural and artificial magnets.
